# Maryland Energy Analysis (1970-2024)

## Overview
This project analyzes Maryland's energy consumption, prices, and expenditures across residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation sectors from 1970 to 2024.

## Dataset
- **Source**: U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)
- **Time Period**: 1970-2024
- **Metrics**: 24 different energy metrics including:
  - Average prices by sector (residential, commercial, industrial, transportation)
  - Expenditures by energy source (petroleum, natural gas, coal, electricity)
  - Total energy consumption
  - Energy consumption per dollar of real GDP
